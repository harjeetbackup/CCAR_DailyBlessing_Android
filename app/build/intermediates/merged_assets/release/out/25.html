FOR A MALE

Baruch atah, Adonai Eloheinu,
Melech haolam,
ham’afshir li lachalok chayai
im rei-ah ahuv v’oheiv.

 

FOR A FEMALE

Baruch atah, Adonai Eloheinu,
Melech haolam,
ham’afshir li lachalok chayai
im rei-ah ahuvah v’ohevet.

 

Praise to You, Adonai our God, Sovereign of the universe: You enable us to share life with a beloved and loving friend.
