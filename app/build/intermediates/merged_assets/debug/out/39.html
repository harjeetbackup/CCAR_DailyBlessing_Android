Leader:

Chaveirim vachaveirot, n’vareich!

Let us praise God.

Group:

Y’hi shem Adonai m’vorach
mei-atah v’ad olam.

Praised be the name of God, now and forever!

Leader:

Y’hi shem Adonai m’vorach mei-atah v’ad olam. Birshut hachevrah, n’vareich Eloheinu she-achalnu mishelo.

Praised be the name of God, now and forever Praised be our God, of whose abundance we have eaten.

Group:

Baruch Eloheinu she-achalnu mishelo uv’tuvo chayinu.

Praised be our God, of whose abundance we have eaten, and by whose goodness we live.

Leader:

Baruch Eloheinu she-achalnu mishelo uv’tuvo chayinu. Baruch hu uvaruch sh’mo.

Praised be our God, of whose abundance we have eaten, and by whose goodness we live. Praised be God and praised be God’s name.
