We give thanks that we are able to fulfill the mitzvah of honoring parents.

Baruch atah, Adonai Eloheinu,
Melech haolam,
shenatan lanu horim ohavim
lismoach bahem.

Praise to You, Adonai our God, Sovereign of the universe: You give us loving parents in whom we rejoice.

Response:

Baruch shenatan lanu
banim y’karim u’vanot y’karot
lismoach bahem.

Praised be the One who gives us precious children in whom we rejoice.
