SHIR HAMAALOT

שִׁיר הַמַּעֲלוֹת, בְּשׁוּב יְהֹוָה אֶת־שִׁיבַת צִיּוֹן, הָיִֽינוּ כְּחֹלְמִֽים. אָז יִמָּלֵא שְׂחוֹק פִּֽינוּ, וּלְשׁוֹנֵֽנוּ רִנָּה. אָז יֹאמְרוּ בַגּוֹיִם׃ הִגְדִּיל יְהֹוָה לַֽעֲשׂוֹת עִם־אֵֽלֶּה. הִגְדִּיל יְהֹוָה לַֽעֲשׂוֹת עִמָּֽנוּ, הָיִֽינוּ שְׂמֵחִים.

Shir hamaalot, b’shuv Adonai et shivat Tziyon hayinu k’chol’mim. Az yimalei s’chok pinu, ul’shoneinu rinah. Az yom’ru vagoyim, higdil Adonai laasot im eileh. Higdil Adonai laasot imanu, hayinu s’meichim.

A song of ascents: When Adonai brought the exiles back to Zion it was like a dream. Then our mouths were filled with laughter and our tongues with song. Then was it said among the nations: “ Adonai has done great things for them.” Truly, Adonai has done great things for us.
