Y’DID NEFESH

יְדִיד נֶֽפֶשׁ, אָב הָרַחֲמָן, מְשׁוֹךְ עַבְדְּךָ אֶל רְצוֹנֶֽךָ׃

יָרוּץ עַבְדְּךָ כְּמוֹ אַיָּל יִשְׁתַּחֲוֶה אֶל מוּל הֲדָרֶֽךָ.

Y’did nefesh, av harachaman, m’shoch avd’cha el r’tzonecha: yarutz avd’cha k’mo ayal yishtachaveh el mul hadarecha.

Beloved of the soul, Source of mercy, draw Your servant into Your arms; I leap like a deer to stand in awe before You.
