HAVDALAH - Eliyahu HaNavi

Extinguish the candle in wine, and sing:

אֵלִיָּֽהוּ הַנָּבִיא, אֵלִיָּֽהוּ הַתִּשְׁבִּי, אֵלִיָּֽהוּ הַגִּלְעָדִי. בִּמְהֵרָה בְיָמֵֽינוּ, יָבוֹא אֵלֵֽינוּ, עִם מָשִֽׁיחַ בֶּן־דָּוִד.

Eliyahu HaNavi, Eliyahu HaTishbi, Eliyahu HaGiladi. Bimheirah v’yameinu, yavo eileinu, im Mashiach ben David.

Elijah the prophet, the Tishbite, the Gileadite: come to us soon, to herald our redemption.
