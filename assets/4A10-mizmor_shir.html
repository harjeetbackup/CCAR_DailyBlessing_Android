MIZMOR SHIR

מִזְמוֹר שִׁיר לְיוֹם הַשַּׁבָּת. טוֹב לְהוֹדוֹת לַיְיָ

וּלְזַמֵּר לְשִׁמְךָ עֶלְיוֹן.

לְהַגִּיד בַּבֹּֽקֶר חַסְדֶּֽךָ, וֶאֱמוּנָתְךָ בַּלֵּילוֹת

עֲלֵי־עָשׂוֹר וַעֲלֵי־נָֽבֶל, עֲלֵי הִגָּיוֹן בְּכִנּוֹר.

Mizmor shir l’yom haShabbat.

Tov l’hodot l’Adonai, ul’zameir l’shimcha Elyon.

L’hagid baboker chasdecha, ve-emunat’cha baleilot.

Alei asor vaalei navel, alei higayon b’chinor.

A psalm, a song for the Shabbat. It is good to give thanks to Adonai , to sing hymns to Your name, O Most High!

To tell of Your loving-kindness in the morning and Your faithfulness at night; to pluck the strings, to sound the lute; to make the harp vibrate.
