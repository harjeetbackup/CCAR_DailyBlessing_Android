WORSHIP ADONAI WITH JOY.

Psalm 100:2

Birkon Mikdash M’at is a bencher—bencher is Yiddish and birkon is Hebrew—a small, portable book of songs, prayers, and blessings. Benchers have been used for generations, but this publication marks the first edition created by NFTY—the North American Federation of Temple Youth. Now CCAR is pleased to offer this bencher as another first — the first digital edition. Contemporary technology allows us to make this content even more portable, by putting it on the devices we already carry with us.

Throughout the Reform Movement—at camps, regional NFTY events, synagogue youth group meetings, and other times of gathering—prayers such as Birkat HaMazon and Shabbat blessings are recited and then followed by communal singing. Birkon Mikdash M’at is designed to facilitate and enhance the joy of singing in groups, the ritual moments of holiday observance, the spiritual experience of Shabbat. This digital edition makes those sacred moments even more possible.

We hope that Birkon Mikdash M’at will find its way into your events, your camps, and your daily Jewish life.
