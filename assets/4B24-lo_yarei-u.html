LO YAREI-U

לֹא יָרֵֽעוּ וְלֹא יַשְׁחִֽיתוּ.

וְכִתְּתוּ חַרְבוֹתָם לְאִתִּים וַחֲנִיתוֹתֵיהֶם לְמַזְמֵרוֹת.

לֹא יִשָּׂא גוֹי אֶל גוֹי חֶֽרֶב, לֹא יִלְמְדוּ עוֹד מִלְחָמָה.

Lo yarei-u v’lo yashchitu. V’chit’tu charvotam l’itim vachanitoteihem l’mazmeirot. Lo yisa goi el goi cherev, lo yilm’du od milchamah.

They shall not hurt or destroy. They shall beat their swords into plowshares, their spears into pruning-hooks. Nation shall not lift up sword against nation, nor ever again shall they train for war.
