Chanukah: She-Asah Nisim

בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, אֱלֹהֵֽינוּ מֶֽלֶךְ הָעוֹלָם, שֶׁעָשָׂה נִסִּים לְדוֹרוֹתֵֽינוּ בַּיָּמִים הָהֵם בַּזְּמַן הַזֶּה.

Baruch atah Adonai, Eloheinu Melech haolam, she-asah nisim l’doroteinu bayamim haheim baz’man hazeh.

Blessed are You, Eternal our God, Sovereign of all, who performed wondrous deeds for our ancestors in days of old at this season.
