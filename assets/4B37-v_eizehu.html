V’EIZEHU

וְאֵיזֶהוּ חָכָם? הַלּוֹמֵד מִכָּל אָדָם.

וְאֵיזֶהוּ גִּבּוֹר? הַכּוֹבֵשׁ אֶת יִצְרוֹ.

וְאֵיזֶהוּ עָשִׁיר? הַשָּׂמֵֽחַ בְּחֶלְקוֹ.

V’eizehu chacham? Halomeid mikol adam. V’eizehu gibor? Hakoveish et yitzro. V’eizehu ashir? Hasamei-ach b’chelko.

Who is wise? Those who learn from all people. Who is strong? Those who control their impulses. Who is rich? Those who are happy with what they have.
