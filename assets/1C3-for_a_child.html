For a Child

Blessed is the parent and blessed is the child

when our hearts are turned toward one another.

Blessed is the home filled with laughter and light,

and joyful spirit of Shabbat.

Blessed is the home when, even in difficult times,

we can fill it with the light of Shabbat.

Tonight I remind you:

Seek truth always.

Speak words of kindness.

Be just and loving in your deeds.

Let Torah be your guide.

A noble heritage has been entrusted to you; guard it well.

May God bless you and protect you.
