ASHREI

אַשְׁרֵי יוֹשְׁבֵי בֵיתֶֽךָ, עוֹד יְהַלְלֽוּךָ סֶֽלָה.

אַשְׁרֵי הָעָם שֶׁכָּֽכָה לּוֹ, אַשְׁרֵי הָעָם שֶׁיְיָ אֱלֹהָיו.

Ashrei yosh’vei veitecha, od y’hal’lucha selah.

Ashrei haam shekachah lo, ashrei haam she-Adonai Elohav.

Happy are those who dwell in Your house; they are always praising You. Happy the people that is so situated; happy the people whose God is the Eternal.
