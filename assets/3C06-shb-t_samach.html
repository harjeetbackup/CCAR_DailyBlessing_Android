SHEVA B’RACHOT : THE SEVEN WEDDING BLESSINGS

#6

שַׂמֵּֽחַ תְּשַׂמַּח רֵעִים הָאֲהוּבִים, כְּשַׂמֵּחֲךָ יְצִירְךָ בְּגַן עֵֽדֶן מִקֶּֽדֶם. בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, מְשַׂמֵּֽחַ חָתָן וְכַלָּה.

Samei-ach t’samach rei-im haahuvim, k’sameichacha y’tzir’cha b’Gan Eiden mikedem. Baruch atah Adonai, m’samei-ach chatan v’chalah.

May these loving companions rejoice together with the joy You have set aside for them since the days of Creation. Blessed are You, Adonai, who grants joy to this couple.
