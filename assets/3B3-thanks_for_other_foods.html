THANKSGIVING FOR OTHER FOODS

When no bread has been eaten, recite this blessing.

בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, אֱלֹהֵֽינוּ מֶֽלֶךְ הָעוֹלָם, בּוֹרֵא נְפָשׁוֹת רַבּוֹת וְחֶסְרוֹנָן עַל כָּל־מַה שֶׁבָּרָֽאתָ לְהַחֲיוֹת בָּהֶם נֶֽפֶשׁ כָּל־חָי. בָּרוּךְ חֵי הָעוֹלָמִים.

Baruch atah Adonai, Eloheinu Melech haolam, borei n’fashot rabot v’chesronan al kol mah shebarata l’hachayot bahem nefesh kol chai. Baruch chei haolamim.

We praise You, Eternal God, Sovereign of the universe, Creator of the living and their needs. Life of all life, we thank You for the food that keeps us alive.
