HARIU L’ADONAI

הָרִֽיעוּ לַיְיָ כָּל־הָאָֽרֶץ! פִּצְחוּ וְרַנְּנוּ וְזַמֵּֽרוּ.

כִּי־טוֹב יְיָ לְעוֹלָם חַסְדּוֹ.

Hariu l’Adonai kol haaretz! Pitzchu v’ran’nu v’zameiru. Ki tov Adonai l’olam chasdo.

Shout joyfully to Adonai , all the earth! Break into song, rejoice, and sing. For Adonai is good and God’s love is everlasting.
