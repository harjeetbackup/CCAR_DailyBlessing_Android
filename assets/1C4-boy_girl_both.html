For a boy

יְשִׂמְךָ אֱלֹהִים כְּאֶפְרַֽיִם וְכִמְנַשֶּׁה.

Y’sim’cha Elohim k’Efrayim v’chiM’nasheh.

May God inspire you to live like Ephraim and Manasseh.

For a girl

יְשִׂמֵךְ אֱלֹהִים כְּשָׂרָה, רִבְקָה, רָחֵל וְלֵאָה.

Y’simeich Elohim k’Sarah, Rivkah, Racheil, v’Lei-ah.

May God inspire you to live like Sarah, Rebekah, Rachel, and Leah.

For both boys and girls

יְבָרֶכְךָ יְהֹוָה וְיִשְׁמְרֶֽךָ.

יָאֵר יְהֹוָה פָּנָיו אֵלֶֽיךָ וִיחֻנֶּֽךָּ.

יִשָּׂא יְהֹוָה פָּנָיו אֵלֶֽיךָ וְיָשֵׂם לְךָ שָׁלוֹם.

Y’varech’cha Adonai v’yishm’recha.

Ya-eir Adonai panav eilecha vichuneka.

Yisa Adonai panav eilecha v’yaseim l’cha shalom.

May God bless you and keep you.

May God’s light shine upon you, and may God be gracious to you.

May you feel God’s Presence within you always, and may you find peace.
