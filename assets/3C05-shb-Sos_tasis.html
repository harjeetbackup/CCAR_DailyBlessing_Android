SHEVA B’RACHOT : THE SEVEN WEDDING BLESSINGS

#5

שׂוֹשׂ תָּשִׂישׂ וְתָגֵל הָעֲקָרָה, בְּקִבּוּץ בָּנֶֽיהָ לְתוֹכָהּ בְּשִׂמְחָה. בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, מְשַׂמֵּֽחַ צִיּוֹן בְּבָנֶֽיהָ.

Sos tasis v’tageil haakarah, b’kibutz baneha l’tochah b’simchah. Baruch atah Adonai, m’samei-ach Tziyon b’vaneha.

Let Zion rejoice at her children’s happy return. Blessed are You, Adonai; You allow those who have sown in tears to reap in joy.
